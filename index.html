About me

Hello, welcome to my website! I am currently a Master student at Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences, Beijing, China. During my Master study, I did a long-term and happy internship in ByteDance AI Lab. Before that, I received my Bachelor’s Degree from Tongji University in 2019.

My mission is to build intelligent embodied agents that can interact with complex and unstructured real-world environments, with application to home robotics. Currently, my studies mainly focus on the perception and manipulation of robot, and apply machine learning techniques to boost them.

My Chinese name is 李一鸣。
